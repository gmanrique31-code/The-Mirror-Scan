THE MIRROR SCAN

This is a conscience check-up. Adult life is coming. Are you building with premium materials or taking shortcuts?

1. The Exam

The teacher leaves the room for 15 minutes during a hard test. Everyone starts sharing answers.

2. The "Unfriendly" Classmate

A classmate you don't like is being treated unfairly by a group.

3. The Group Error

You notice a mistake in a project 5 min before handing it in. It was your fault.

4. AI vs. Learning

You have a complex essay. You can use AI to do 100% of the work.

5. The Gossip

Your friends are destroying someone's reputation with a rumor.

6. Quality Control

A classmate asks for feedback. Their work is poor and they will fail.

7. Infrastructure

You see someone damaging school property (laptops/furniture) for fun.
